Why Shambhavi

Abstract is easy. Specific is harder. Four differences you can measure.

I.

Two seconds to intervention.

When a student on AspiremsAI signals distress in their journal, the KIRAN helpline appears on their screen in under two seconds — before any human has read a word. A counselor is alerted, a safety flag goes live on the admin dashboard. That is what AI-native means when it matters.

II.

19 modules. 4 countries. One login.

Sanadeep runs payroll across India (PF, ESI, Form 16), the UAE (WPS), the UK (P60), and the US (W-2) from a single platform — with Arabic right-to-left support built in. Most enterprise HRMS companies treat multi-country as a roadmap. We treated it as the foundation.

III.

3.9 lakh students, every year.

That is how many CS engineering students India admits annually — and most graduate without ever building a real model. NeuroLink v2 gives each of them a live Concept DNA knowledge graph, a personalized lesson from every PDF their professor uploads, and zero GPU infrastructure to manage.

IV.

60 to 70 percent less QA effort.

That is how much faster InsightTest AI ships test coverage than hand-coded QA. Drag-and-drop builder, AI-generated cases, self-healing when your UI shifts. One platform for mobile, web, API, and analytics — instead of five.
