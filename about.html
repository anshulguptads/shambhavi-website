Shambhavi Labs / About
शाम्भवी दृष्टि

We were built on a simple idea: that everyone deserves access to the same quality of tools.

Shambhavi Innovation Lab is an India-born company building AI-native platforms for learning, working, and building — because the gap between those who have great technology and those who don't has never been wider, and we intend to close it.

Our Mission

To build something for everyone — so that everyone can learn, grow, and build together.

Our Story

From an idea in 2023 to four live platforms in 2026.

2023

The first conversations.

The idea for Shambhavi Innovation Lab began in conversations — between educators, technologists, and young people who felt the Indian education system had fundamentally stopped listening to its students. We started with one question: what would it take for every student, not just the privileged, to have a real mentor?

2024

Building, quietly.

We began prototyping AspiremsAI in small schools and with early pilot groups. What started as a tutor became something more — a persona engine, a crisis detection system, a full learning management platform. We spent a year listening to students, teachers, and parents, and rebuilding based on what we heard.

2025

The thesis expanded.

If AI could meaningfully transform how a school student learns, it could transform every stage of human development. We began building for universities (NeuroLink v2), for the workplace (Sanadeep), and for the engineering teams that ship software to the rest of the world (InsightTest AI). Four products. One conviction.

2026

Shambhavi Innovation Lab, officially.

Shambhavi Innovation Lab was formally incorporated in 2026 — the legal beginning of what has been, in practice, three years of work. AspiremsAI is live in schools. Sanadeep is deployed. NeuroLink v2 is in pilot. InsightTest AI is shipping to engineering teams. The Lab's work has just begun.

Aman Verma
Co-Founder
Product & Vision Shambhavi Innovation Lab

Since childhood, I have envisioned myself as a mentor — one who guides not by authority, but through understanding. My purpose has always been to reach beyond judgments and help others discover clarity, confidence, and meaning in their own stories.

Through MUNs, workshops, and countless sessions of heartfelt interaction, I realized that mentorship is not about teaching people what to think, but how to think — and how to become. That realization planted the seed for a new idea: a space where learning, working, and building could all feel deeply human, yet future-driven.

From that seed, Shambhavi Innovation Lab was born — not merely as a company, but as a belief that every student, every professional, every builder, when guided with empathy and purpose, can become a star in their own journey.

What Drives Us

The values that hold our products together.

01

Access, not privilege.

Every product we build starts from one question: can this reach the person who would benefit most, not just the one who can afford most? We don't build for the few. We build for the many who have been left out, and we let the few come along.

02

AI in service of humans.

Our products use AI to amplify human potential — the student who wants to learn, the manager who wants to lead, the engineer who wants to ship. The moment AI stops serving the person, it stops being intelligence. It becomes surveillance.

03

Specific over abstract.

We prefer a real feature that works today to a grand claim that might ship eventually. Our products are measured against things we can prove — crisis response times, test effort saved, statutory filings completed. Abstract is easy. Specific is what we sell.

04

India at the center.

Hindi, Arabic RTL, DPDPA, WPS, PF, Form 16 — the regulatory and linguistic realities other companies treat as an afterthought, we treat as the foundation. India isn't a market for us. It's the ground we stand on.

05

Build what you wish existed.

Every product at Shambhavi started with someone on our team saying: "I wish this had existed when I was in school / at that job / on that team." The best products come from founders who are solving their own unmet needs, at scale, for everyone else like them.

06

Ship with empathy.

Our products touch students in distress, employees at risk, engineers at 2 AM. We build with the awareness that behind every feature is a real human having a real moment. Empathy isn't a soft value. It's an engineering requirement.
